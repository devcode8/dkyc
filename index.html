Services

One stop solution for all your KYC troubles.

Distrubted Ledger

Distributed ledger technology provides a single source of truth for your database. The cryptographic framework of the block-chain ensures that your data cannot be tampered with, thus providing a secure source for compliance checks.

Viewership

The data stored is only accessible to the banks, only if the owner permits to do so

Sharing

The data can be shared to other banks in the consortium only after the request is approved by the user

Onboarding Help

If you are new to our platform, follow the steps below to get yourself registered on the consortium

Login

Create an account with Bank Name,IFSC code, Password should be public address of the wallet

Add Records

Login with your credentials, Enter the details and pay the gas fees to add the record to the blockchain

Retreiving Records

Retreival of data is in the hands of the customer, banks can only view the records after the view request has been approved by the customer
